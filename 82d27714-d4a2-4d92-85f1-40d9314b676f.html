How "estimated annual value" is calculated: reward earnings across your 5 spend categories (applying each card's actual rates and monthly caps × 12) minus the annual fee you'd actually pay after any spend-based waiver. Your chosen priority category counts 1.5× toward ranking, but the ₹ value shown is the real unweighted total. Point-based cards use each issuer's own stated redemption rate (statement credit / standard catalogue) — not optimistic airline-transfer values — so real-world results can be higher if you redeem strategically.
